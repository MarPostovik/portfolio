Me and hip-hop

After I quit aerobics, I started hip hop dancing. There was a very friendly team and a warm atmosphere. Our team took part in the competition, in which we won the grand prize and the video from which is presented on this website. I danced for half a year, until I found programming courses and decided to choose them, because I physically did not have enough time to do both. But I continue to do sports activities, namely going to group fitness classes to keep fit.
