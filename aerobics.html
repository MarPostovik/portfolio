Me and sports aerobics

Sports aerobics was a huge part of my life. I did it almost every day for about 7.5 years. I participated in many competitions. In 2021 received the title of "Candidate for Master of Sports in Sports Aerobics". But I can't say that all this time I went to training with pleasure. No. It was difficult for me and I know what you would say: "You just had to work harder." I tried, I worked, but I understood that it's just not mine, that I don't want to develop in it, I don't want to connect my future with it. My parents wanted me to go there. And I was afraid to disappoint them. But in the winter of 2022, I realized that I had enough. I will no longer do what others want me to do. This is my life and I don't want to waste it on something I don't like. And I quit aerobics. More than a year has passed since then, and I have never once regretted the decision I made. Since then, I do only what brings me pleasure.
